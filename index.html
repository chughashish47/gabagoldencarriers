About Us

Four Decades of Moving India Forward

Established in 1985 in Faridabad, Haryana by Mr. Varinder Gaba (Managing Director), with Mr. Darshan Gaba joining as Director in 1999, Gaba Golden Carriers Private Limited was built to provide excellent logistics solutions — assuring in-time, safe delivery of goods, every time.

It is our endeavour to serve our customers with unparalleled speed, safety and customised service. We intend to exceed our customers' expectations, every single trip.

  • GST Registered Limited Company
  • 11–25 dedicated logistics professionals
  • Trusted by leading two-wheeler & FMCG brands
  • Pan-India door-to-door full truck load service

Our Vision

To operate an innovative and disciplined logistics company by adopting the latest business practices, policies and technology to meet our customers' requirements.

Our Mission

Provide logistics services with safe and on-time deliveries, while maintaining a safe workplace and upholding integrity, fairness and honesty with every customer.
